
Andromeda SL
============

Free OpenType font 
from Silesian Letters
http://www.silesian.com/

Andromeda SL font may be distributed in original form and used free of
charge for any purpose. Modifications require author's permission. All
rights reserved.

The font Andromeda SL was inspired by a 1970's-like logo design for
the "Andromeda" cinema in Tychy, Poland. Font includes the "Andromeda"
logo at the trademark character. Font contains accented characters for
many Western and Central European languages.
